Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef with the chopped onion and minced garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and egg. Mix well.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Place a layer of noodles on top.
  7. Spread some of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
